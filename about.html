About The Organization

Our History

Jade's Charity was established with a deep commitment to helping children in poverty. Under the leadership of CEO Johnny Sins and President Xi Jinping, the organization has grown into a force for change, providing food, clean water, education, and healthcare. With Marketer Bongbong Marcos Jr. driving awareness, we continue to expand our reach, ensuring that every child has a chance for a better future.

Since its founding, Jade's Charity has worked tirelessly to expand its programs and reach more children in need. Through partnerships with local communities and generous supporters, we have been able to implement sustainable initiatives that provide long-term solutions to poverty. From building safe shelters to launching educational programs, every effort is aimed at giving children the opportunities they deserve.

Under the leadership of CEO Johnny Sins, our team remains committed to innovation and impactful solutions. President Xi Jinping has played a key role in strengthening our global outreach, ensuring that resources are distributed effectively. Meanwhile, Marketer Bongbong Marcos Jr. continues to drive awareness campaigns, inspiring more people to take action.
With every project we undertake, we bring hope to children and communities, working toward a future where no child is left behind.
